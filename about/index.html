About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Queen Mary, University of London. I am currently working on communicating uncertainty, work funded by the DSTL.

I am also a Visiting Fellow in Warwick Business School at the University of Warwick.

Generally, I am interested in investigating learning, categorisation and decision-making. That being said, I also collaborate on projects investigating Human-Robot Interaction. (Mainly focusing on the “human” aspect!)
